TSJ price

Build directions with clearer budget levels.

This page gives a simple price overview for different scooter directions. It can later grow into a more detailed guide with parts, labor, and custom options.

Price categories

From clean basics to full racing setups.

These are starting points for the site and can be changed later to your real prices.

01 / Stock

Basic refresh

€300 - €700

A simple clean build direction for riders who want the scooter looking better without going deep into performance.

  • Visual cleanup
  • Basic maintenance
  • Original-style setup

02 / Sport

Street upgrade

€700 - €1,500

A more active setup with stronger response, better road feel, and a more noticeable build identity.

  • Light performance parts
  • Sharper visual details
  • Better street balance

03 / Sport Pro

Serious build

€1,500 - €3,000

A bigger budget direction with more performance focus, more custom choices, and a stronger finished result.

  • Focused setup package
  • Better tuning direction
  • Premium custom parts

04 / Racing

Top-level setup

€3,000+

The most aggressive route for riders who want a highly serious machine with race-inspired direction and performance-first parts.

  • Maximum performance focus
  • Advanced parts selection
  • Race-style build logic

What can affect price

Every build can move depending on the parts and the goal.

01

Parts quality

Premium components, special trims, and stronger brands always push the build higher.

02

Visual work

Paint, body details, cleanup, and finishing can change the final price a lot.

03

Performance level

The more speed and setup focus you want, the more expensive the whole direction becomes.

04

Custom requests

Rare parts, unique ideas, and one-off solutions can move a scooter into a different range.
