About us

We are a scooter crew built on friendship, taste, and ambition.

Who we are

TSJ is three friends who share the same interest in scooters, visual identity, and turning ideas into something real.

What we believe

We believe even a simple scooter can become something memorable when the direction, details, and style are right.

What drives us

Our goal is to keep building, improving, and shaping a stronger identity around the things we create together.

What we do

We buy old scooters, rebuild them, and give them a cleaner future.

We take scooters that have lost their value or their look, restore them, reshape them, and create custom builds with more presence, more character, and a stronger visual direction.
