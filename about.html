About TSJ

One direction, one evolving scooter identity.

TSJscootbuild is a crew built around friendship, scooter culture, restoration, and the desire to make old machines look sharper, cleaner, and more memorable.

Who we are

We care about more than parts. We care about direction.

We buy old scooters, restore them, build custom setups, and shape a style that feels deliberate. Every project should look like it has a reason behind it.
